Projects

Care NCCU

June 2023

A chatbot for NCCU students to access mental health counseling information.
I implemented ChatGPT API for generating conversational content and leveraged Dialogflow for analyzing user input. It supports features including depression self test and providing counseling services information.

Care NCCU aims to provide a reliable mental health information channel to assist in addressing the 69% increase in counseling demand among NCCU students over the past three years.

Noisserpmi

May 2023

Noisserpmi is my work for the NCCU DCT 14th graduation exhibition.
The work consists of four interactive artworks.

I developed interactive web pages by p5.js, HTML/CSS, Express, Socket.IO
I also implemented various Arduino sensors to collect data to trigger interactive elements on web pages using Johnny-Five library.

Wachine Machine Helper

Dec. 2022

I led my team to develop a helper system for monitoring washing machine availability in university dormitory. We implemented buttons and reed switches to track the status of washing machines, and also developed a Node.js-based website integrated with the sensor module using Johnny-Five library.

We implemented our prototype system in one of the NCCU dormitory and succesully improved the issue of laundry rush.

Save the Reefs

Jan. 2022

I collaborated with two members to develop a web-based game to support the algal reef protection referendum in Taiwan. I implemented a Node.js-based website with p5.js for visualization and Socket.IO for multiplayer functionality.

Our game was played by 50 students and we successfully raised environmental awareness among these players by integrating educational elements into the game.
