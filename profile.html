Care NCCU

June 2023

A chatbot for NCCU students to access mental health counseling
information. It supports features including depression self test
and providing counseling services information.

Care NCCU aims to provide a mental health information through
conversational content, assisting in the 69% increase in
counseling demand among NCCU students over the past three years.

Noisserpmi

May 2023

Noisserpmi is my work for the NCCU DCT 14th graduation exhibition.
This artwork consists of four interactive art installations, and I designed
three of them.

I developed the interactive websites with p5.js, Express, Socket.IO,
and integrated data from accelerometers on mobile devices and Arduino
sensors through Johnny-Five library to trigger interactive elements.

(Secret: Noisserpmi is the opposite of Impression)

Wachine Machine Helper

Dec. 2022

I led my team to develop a helper system for monitoring washing machine availability in university dormitory. We implemented buttons and reed switches to track the status of washing machines, and developed a Node.js-based website integrated with the sensor module using Johnny-Five library.

We implemented our prototype system in one of the NCCU dormitory and succesully improved the issue of laundry rush.
