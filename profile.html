Care NCCU

June 2023

A chatbot for NCCU students to access mental health counseling information. There has been a 69% increase in counseling demand among NCCU students over the past three years. In respond to the issue, Care NCCU serves as a platform to provide students with information and resources.

It supports features including depression self test and providing counseling services information.
